How Super works with Meta Display

1. Pair Meta Display
Sign in, open Settings, and pair Meta Display so Super can show replies and task notifications. Setup guide.
2. Speak or tap
Use voice or subtle gestures. Super listens, plans, and asks for confirmation when needed.
3. Computer use
Super opens a browser or connected device, clicks, types, scrolls, and captures results.
4. Reuse the computer-use cache
Repeated tasks benefit from the same computer-use cache, reducing friction on future runs.
